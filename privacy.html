BabyTrack Terms of Use Support

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026.

BabyTrack is a local video effects app. It lets you choose a video from Photos or Files, preview tracking effects, and export a processed video to Photos.

Data Collection

BabyTrack does not collect personal data, analytics data, advertising identifiers, or usage data.

Videos and Photos

Videos selected in BabyTrack are processed on your device. BabyTrack does not upload your original videos or exported videos to a server. Exported videos are saved to Photos only when you choose to export.

Permissions

BabyTrack requests Photos access so you can choose a video and save the finished export. Access is limited to the actions you approve through iOS.

Third Parties

BabyTrack does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account services, or cloud sync services.

Contact

For support, use the public BabyTrack support page linked from the App Store listing.
