Support

BabyTrack is a local video effects app for turning existing videos into motion tracking clips.

Common Questions

Where are my videos processed?

Videos are processed on your device. BabyTrack does not upload originals or exports to a server.

Why does BabyTrack ask for Photos access?

BabyTrack uses Photos access only to choose videos you select and save finished exports when you tap Export Video.

Why did export fail?

Try a shorter clip first, confirm the video can play in Photos or Files, and make sure BabyTrack is allowed to add videos to Photos in iOS Settings.

Does BabyTrack require an account?

No. BabyTrack does not use accounts, subscriptions, ads, analytics, or cloud sync.
